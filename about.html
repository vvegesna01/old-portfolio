About Me

Hi there! I'm Keerthana Vegesna, and I'm a junior in Computer Science at Purdue University.


I'm an incoming Software Engineering Intern at Cummins, Inc. I am an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant for The Data Mine at Purdue. I'm also an Undergraduate Student Researcher for Merck as part of The Data Mine's Corporate Partners Program. I enjoy contributing to the Computer Science communities at Purdue and have been involved with Girls Who Code - Purdue University and the CS Bridge Program as a Teaching Assistant in the past.
